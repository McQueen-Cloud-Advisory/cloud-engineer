# Career

## Purpose

This section helps you turn cloud engineering learning into portfolio artifacts, resume evidence, and interview-ready explanations. The goal is to present technical work clearly and credibly.

## What You Should Understand

- Projects become more valuable when you can explain the problem, architecture, tradeoffs, and operating concerns.
- Portfolio material should show decisions and outcomes, not only screenshots or service names.
- Resume bullets and interview stories should connect technical work to business or delivery impact.

Cloud engineering skill is partly technical execution and partly technical communication. This section helps you describe what you built, why it mattered, and how you operated it responsibly.
