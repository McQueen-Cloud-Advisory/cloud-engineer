# Observability

## Purpose

This page explains how logs, metrics, traces, dashboards, and alerts help teams understand system behavior and respond to failures.

## What You Should Understand

- Monitoring and observability should be designed with the system, not added after an incident.
- Logs, metrics, and traces answer different questions and work best together.
- Alert quality matters more than alert quantity.
## How This Fits Into Cloud Engineering

Cloud engineers rely on observability to debug issues, measure service health, and prove that changes are improving the system. Weak observability slows every other engineering activity.
